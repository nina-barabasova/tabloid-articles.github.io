Everyone loves desserts. The sweet taste of whipped cream melting on your tongue is simply delish! Luckily for GJH students, our beloved buffet was revamped and looks better than ever!

Now called Bufet Lienka 1, the buffet is filled with a wide variety of pastries, sandwiches and snacks. This scrumptious sanctuary can be found in the vestibule of the school. Its muffin and sandwich stickers make it impossible to miss.

Bufet Lienka 1 has a wide ranging selection of foods. If you’re feeling zesty, you are bound to like the one of many baguette variants. Their flavours span from tuna to chicken – all under two euros each!

If you have a big sweet tooth, the pasties are to die for. Packed with eye-popping donuts and an array of different sweets, chocolate bars and biscuits, you are sure to find something you like. Not to mention all the different drinks worth a snag!

So come on over to your local Bufet Lienka 1 and take your taste buds on an out worldly experience you’re not to forget!
